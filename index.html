A Nowruz Party

Nowruz, the Persian New Year, is celebrated on the spring equinox and marks the beginning of a new year filled with hope and renewal. Traditions include setting up a Haft-sin table, symbolizing health, wealth, and prosperity, and spending time with loved ones to reflect on the past year and look forward to the future.

The celebration will feature delicious Persian food and drinks, including Sabzi Polo ba Mahi, Ghormeh Sabzi, Kashke Bademjun, and Gheimeh, along with live traditional music to create a festive and joyful atmosphere.
